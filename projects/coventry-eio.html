Coventry University EIO Strategy & Consultancy

Shaping product roadmaps and revenue growth through strategic market analysis.

Project Overview

Led a strategic consultancy project for Coventry University’s commercial division (EIO), addressing critical business challenges and reporting directly to the Director of Business & Product Development. This work directly informed the division’s strategic roadmap.

Key Contributions

  • Conducted market and competitor analysis using Ansoff Matrix, Porter’s Five Forces, and SWOT.
  • Analyzed CPD product offerings contributing 45–50% of revenue.
  • Identified expansion opportunities in Sustainability, AI, and Mobility.
  • Designed interactive dashboards in Excel and Power BI to monitor competitor activity and market trends.
  • Provided strategic recommendations potentially increasing revenue by 15-20% Y-o-Y.

Business Impact

The project resulted in a streamlined workflow adopted across departments and provided a data-backed foundation for new hybrid academic commercial offerings and business models.
