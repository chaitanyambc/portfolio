Enterprise Transport System (TMS) Data Analysis

Optimizing logistics and operational efficiency through data-driven insights.

Project Overview

Conducted data analysis for an Enterprise Transport System (TMS), focusing on understanding logistics patterns and identifying areas for operational improvement. This project involved handling large-scale transport data to drive better decision-making in supply chain management.

Key Contributions

  • Extracted and cleaned transport data from enterprise systems.
  • Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify bottlenecks in the transport lifecycle.
  • Designed KPIs to monitor vehicle utilization and delivery performance.
  • Transformed raw data into actionable insights for logistics managers.

Business Impact

The analysis helped the business understand its transport performance more clearly, enabling better resource allocation and identifying cost-saving opportunities within the logistics network.
